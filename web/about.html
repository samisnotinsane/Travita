About Trivita

OUR MISSION

Jay, Kate, Kristina and Sam developed a solution to link frequent flyers with charities in destination countries. Many of these charities need items that are often cheaper to purchase abroad than locally. And as frequent flyers often donâ€™t use up their full baggage allowance, there would be room for these items to be transported to the charities in a true win-win situation.

Many travellers want to support charities but want to know where their donations go. With Travita they are able to buy exactly what the charity needs with no administration costs. Furthermore, with the share feature on Travita they are able to broadcast to their friends and followers about their good deed via Twitter or Facebook. A big plus for many businesses who want to demonstrate that they do their bit but ensure that their money goes to the right cause.
